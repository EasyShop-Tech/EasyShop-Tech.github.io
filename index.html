Who we are

Our vision is a Smart and Connected retail ecosystem for Africa. We offer an online marketing platform that connects retailers and shoppers digitally. Our product enables retailers to gain consumer insights and allow shoppers access to the latest retail pricing information. The aim of EasySHOP is to provide retailers with an affordable, online marketing tool so that shoppers can know the latest retail offerings. Retailers can use EasySHOP to:

  • plan for and place promotions
  • view the number of shoppers who saw the promotions
  • track shopper movement within their store
  • identify loyal shoppers from new shoppers and push targeted advertising.
