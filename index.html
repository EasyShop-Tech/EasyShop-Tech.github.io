About

Who we are

We are a digital promotions and marketing company building smart retail systems for bricks and mortar retailers. Our vision is a Smart and Connected retail ecosystem for Africa.

  • We offer an online marketing platform that connects retailers and shoppers digitally.
  • Our product enables retailers to gain consumer insights and allow shoppers access to the latest retail pricing information.
  • The Easyshop Mobile Marketing platform is designed to simplify retail promotions’ marketing and communications.
  • The Easyshop Mobile Marketing platform is designed to simplify retail promotions’ marketing and communications.
The solution is multi-sided. Retailers engage with the Retail Managers Portal on which they plan for and place retail promotions and engage with consumers and floor staff, while the shoppers use the Easyshop Mobile Application to be notified of promotions and specials, track their spending and plan for their shopping journey in advance. With EasySHOP you can choose the category of products and shops whose promotions to be notified about.
